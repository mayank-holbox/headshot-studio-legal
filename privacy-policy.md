# Privacy Policy – Headshot Studio AI

Last updated: January 2026

Headshot Studio AI ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## Information We Process

The App allows users to upload images for the sole purpose of generating professional AI-based headshots.

Uploaded images are transmitted securely to our processing servers, used only to generate the requested headshot, and automatically deleted after processing. Generated images are also removed after delivery and are not stored permanently.

---

## Data Collection

Headshot Studio AI does **not** collect, store, or share personal data.

- No user accounts are created
- No analytics or tracking technologies are used
- No advertising SDKs are integrated
- No personal identifiers (such as name, email, or device ID) are collected

All image processing is performed in real time and data is retained only as long as necessary to complete the requested operation.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App may communicate with backend services strictly to perform image processing. These services act solely as data processors and do not retain or reuse images or data for any other purpose.

No third-party analytics, marketing, or advertising partners are used.

---

## Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect data during transmission. Images are transmitted over secure connections and deleted automatically after processing is complete.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Headshot Studio AI does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. The App is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
